Evidence description

From this session, we discussed who our target group would be. We debated whether our target audience should be Generation Z or Generation Alpha. On the whiteboard shown in the attached image, you can see various topics that could generate conflict between people, such as religion, racism, and politics.

One of the proposed ideas was to create a concept in which all participants must work together to open a door and exit the escape room together, symbolizing the need for unity and cooperation to converse, achieve results, or find an object while expressing their opinions.

SCRUM 0, Concept Brainstorming


During Scrum 0, we started with a group brainstorming session to develop three different concepts we could present in our presentation. We came up with a number of ideas for how the concepts should appear during this portion of our conversation. The concepts should be fun and allow two users to have a conversation about topics that aren't too polarizing, such as topics that might cause more conflict when each person wants to express their opinion.
